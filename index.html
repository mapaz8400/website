About Me

My name is Melody Paz, I am currently an ACE student at Florida Gulf Coast University. I was born in Van Nuys, CA., but I was raised in an extremely small town called Labelle, in Florida. I am a Software Engineering major with a minor in graphic designing. As much as I enjoy programming and software, my other hobbies include traveling, drawing, reading, and binge watching conspiracy theories.

My fasination with technology began the day my father brought home a bright red iMac G3. As a two year old, I have a very vague memory, I just remember being mesmeorized by the bright screen and complicated wiring seen through the translucent monitor. From then on, computers have been a huge part of my life and nothing excites me more than seeing all the progress we've made in technology.
